Membership

The SILENCE Circle

A free membership for the people who make sound. Join the Circle and the whole of SILENCE opens up to you — earlier, quieter, kinder on the wallet, and rich with the wisdom of those who came before.

Home/The SILENCE Circle

There are no tiers to climb and no points to chase. The SILENCE Circle is one free account that grants member pricing, first sight of every drop, delivery on us, and a growing library of recorded wisdom from engineers, players and producers who have spent a lifetime listening. Sign up once, and the advantage is yours for as long as you keep making things worth hearing.

Early access to drops

Limited studio gear and capsule apparel move fast. Circle members get the link before anyone else, so the piece you have been waiting for lands in your bag rather than slipping past in silence.

Member pricing

See the prices the crowd does not. Members unlock quieter rates across microphones, monitors, accessories and apparel — plus first entry to every seasonal markdown before it goes public.

Free shipping

Every order ships free, every time — no minimum spend, no fine print. Order a cable at midnight or a full rack on a whim; we cover the journey to your door.

Wisdom library & masterclasses

The heart of the Circle. Stream recorded masterclasses and intimate sessions where mastering engineers, touring players and producers share what took them decades to learn — gain staging, room treatment, the patience of a good take.

Community access

Join a quiet, considered room of fellow musicians. Trade signal chains, swap demos, ask the question you were too shy to ask — a place built on craft and generosity, not noise.

Birthday reward

We mark the date too. A member-only discount arrives in your inbox during your birthday month — a small nod toward the upgrade you have had your eye on all year.

Ready to listen with us?

Joining the Circle takes under a minute and costs nothing. Become a member today and start unlocking pricing, drops and the wisdom library straight away. Already one of us? Sign in and pick up where you left off.
