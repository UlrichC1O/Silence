Why SILENCE

We back our people with the time, tools and trust to do the best work of their careers. No theatre, no busywork — just the conditions that let good people do quiet, lasting work.

Competitive pay & equity

Salaries benchmarked to the top quartile of the market, reviewed every year, plus share options for everyone. When the brand grows, you own a real part of it.

Full gear allowance

A generous yearly credit toward the studio gear, apparel and accessories you help shape. The sharpest feedback we get comes from people who actually live with the work.

Flexible & remote

Work where your ears are happiest — home studio, our space, or somewhere in between. Hybrid by default, fully remote where the role allows, and core hours that respect your focus.

Learning budget

€1,500 a year for courses, residencies, conferences and tools for your craft — yours to spend on getting better at what you love, with no approval gauntlet.

Health & wellbeing

Private medical and dental cover, on-demand mental health support, hearing care for everyone, and quiet days when you need them. Good work starts with rest.

30 days holiday

Thirty days of paid leave on top of public holidays, plus the whole company offline for a week each winter. We measure the work, never the hours at a desk.

How we hire

Three honest conversations, no trick questions and feedback at every step. Most people go from first call to offer in under three weeks.

A relaxed 30 minutes with someone from the team to swap stories — your work, what moves you and what you want next. You will leave knowing exactly what the role involves and who you would work alongside.
A practical session built around real problems we are actually solving — a portfolio walkthrough, a mix to dissect, an editing brief or a working session, never a whiteboard puzzle. A two-way look at how we think together.
Final conversations with your future teammates and a leader, focused on values, taste and the way we work. We aim to send a clear decision within two working days — yes or no, always with reasons.
